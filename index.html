ALL ABOUT MAPPIN' IT

Mappin' It is a communally crowdsourced digital map of Singapore for Migrant Domestic Workers (MDWs) to share about the different places in Singapore they frequent and what it means to them.

MDWs, MDW organisations and non-MDW members of the public can "Place A Pin" on a specific location on the map. Under the pin, users can write a short note and/or upload photos and videos about the place to share more about their experience of that specific place.

This website started as a student-led initiative by a group of 4 students from the National University of Singapore's Tembusu College, under the course UTS2115 Migrant Workers, Rhetoric and Performance.

Our mission is to amplify the voices of MDWs in Singapore by creating a living, breathing archive of the spaces they call their own вҖ” places of joy, community, and belonging in a city far from home.

HOW TO USE MAPPIN' IT

A friendly step-by-step guide to navigating our interactive map

Navigating the Map

1
Zoom in & out using your mouse scroll wheel, trackpad pinch, or the + / вҲ’ buttons at the bottom-left of the map. The map is restricted to Singapore.
2
Pan and drag the map by clicking and dragging to explore different parts of Singapore.
3
At the overview level, you'll see three key locations labelled on the map: Lucky Plaza, Peninsula Plaza, and City Plaza. Click their labels or pins to zoom in.

Understanding the Pins

Filtering Pins

1
Use the Filter Dropdown at the top-right of the map to isolate pins by category вҖ” All Pins, Archive, Upcoming Events, or any specific category.
2
Archive shows past events and initiatives from organisations and moderators. Upcoming Events shows planned future activities.

Viewing Contributions

1
Click on any coloured pin on the map to open the contributions panel on the left side of the screen.
2
Click the вҖә arrow next to any contribution to open its full detail вҖ” including photos, videos, and the full written story.
3
Users with a blue tick are verified members. Users marked (Public) have contributed without a registered account.

Placing a Pin

1
Click on the map at your chosen spot. A red pin will drop to mark your selected location.
2
Click the "PLACE A PIN" button at the bottom-right corner of the screen.
3
In the form, type the location name, choose a category for your pin, upload any photos or videos, then add a title and write about your experience.
4
If you are a Guest, you will also be asked to enter your name. Your contribution will appear with a (Public) label.
5
Click "PLACE A PIN" to publish. Your pin will appear on the map and be clickable by everyone!

Your Profile & Account

1
Click your name/avatar in the top-right corner to access your profile, or use the dropdown menu to log out.
2
When logged out, click Login in the top-right. Your past account is remembered вҖ” click it to sign back in instantly, or continue as a Guest.
3
On your profile, click Edit Name to change your display name. All your contributions will update automatically.
4
Click your profile picture to open the avatar editor вҖ” choose an emoji, upload a photo, or remove your picture entirely.
